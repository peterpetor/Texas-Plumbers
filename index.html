About Jacob & The Team

Texas Plumbers, LLC is a proud Hispanic-owned business dedicated to providing top-tier plumbing services to the Elkhart community and beyond.

Led by Jacob, our philosophy is simple: do the job right the first time, treat the customer's home with respect, and charge a fair price. We believe in building long-term relationships rather than making quick profits. From emergency calls in the dead of night to scheduled maintenance for property managers, we are here for you.
